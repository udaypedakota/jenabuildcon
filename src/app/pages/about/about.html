Who We Are

Expertise in Puri & Beyond

Jenabuildcon Prime Developers is one of Odisha's most trusted real estate developers. With over 25 years of experience, we have transformed thousands of acres of land into premium residential communities that stand as landmarks of quality and trust.

Our journey began with a simple belief — that every family deserves a home built with integrity, craftsmanship, and care. Today, that belief drives every project we undertake, from plotting to luxury villas.
